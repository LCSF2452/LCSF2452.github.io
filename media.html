Norelco Electric Shavers

In the 1940's, a company named Philco(1892) was legally able to prevent Philips(1939) from using Philips in the USA.
Philips used Norelco "North American Philips [electrical] Company" instead.
In 1981, Philips bought Philco and was able to use Philips in the U.S.

Remington Electric Shavers

Remington started as a firearms company and occasionally sewing machines and farm implements. Inventor Christopher Sholes persuaded the company to help him develop the typewriter and QWERTY keyboard.
This led the company to turn into a typewriter company, to later be sold, where it became an office equipment company. They then branched out to electrical shavers.
