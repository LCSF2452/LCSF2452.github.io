How to Clean Your Electric Shaver:

Daily Cleaning (after each use):

  1. Turn off the shaver and unplug it (if corded).
  2. Open the shaving head or foil and remove trapped hair using a small cleaning brush (often provided).
  3. If the shaver is waterproof, rinse the head under running water. Use mild soap for deeper cleaning.
  4. Shake off the excess water and let it air dry before reassembling.

Weekly Maintenance:

  • Lubricate the blades with a drop of shaver oil to reduce friction and wear.
  • Disassemble the cutting elements and clean inside the head housing for thorough maintenance.

Monthly or Deep Cleaning:

  • Use a specialized shaver cleaning solution or system (if provided with the shaver model).
  • Replace worn-out blades, foils, or cutters as recommended by the manufacturer (typically every 12-18 months).
