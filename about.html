Who We Are

We are Electra Shavers. Here we are committed to helping individuals, families and professionals with grooming experience to make their daily routine easier and more efficient.
We offer a carefully selected range of electric shavers designed to meet the needs of individuals, salons and other businesses. Whether you need a personal grooming solution or you're looking to make a bulk order, we are committed to helping deliver the products and providing a smooth, comfortable shave for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, I found that trying to find shavers that worked for me and my family was hard with so many options. Our journey started with the idea of finding out if there is a difference between named brands and generic electric shavers. This question sparked our mission to give others a chance to find an electric shaver that works for them.
