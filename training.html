Training Content

Length:

Different products require a different amount of time to discuss. We may need to divulge some products more than others.

Due to our research on the best electric shavers, we have lots of different types from different companies, some well known and some are not.

Lesson:

Learn about the product of the day. We will teach you all about the product from our product code to the description of the product.

Content types:

We use various kinds of content to give you the full picture of each product, we use a short PowerPoint along with a video of the product, infographics and lots of images.

Assessments:

We will have a short quiz at the end of training for our records and to find ways to improve future training.
